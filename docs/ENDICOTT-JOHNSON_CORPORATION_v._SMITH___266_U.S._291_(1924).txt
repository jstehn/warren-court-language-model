 Mr. Justice SANFORD delivered the opinion of the Court.
 This case, like Endicott-Johnson Corporation v. Encyclopedia Press, Inc. (No. 41), 
266 
U.S. 285
, 45 S. Ct. 61, 69 L. Ed. --, just decided, involves the constitutional validity of section 1391 of the New York Code of Civil Procedure. Smith v. Endicott-Johnson Corporation (Sup.) 189 N. Y. S. 673; Id., 199 App. Div. 194, 192 N. Y. S. 121; Id., 234 N. Y. 628, 138 N. E. 474. It is in all respects similar to the Encyclopedia Press Case and governed by the decision therein.
 The judgment is
 Affirmed.
