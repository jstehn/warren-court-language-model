 THE CHIEF JUSTICE. The decree below enjoined appellants from enforcing against appellees Act No. 71 of the Extra Session of the General Assembly of Louisiana of 1870 (Laws La. Ex. Sess. 1870, p. 156). This act was repealed June 28, 1892 (Acts La. 1892, Act No. 23, p. 34), and the appeal is dismissed, on the authority of Mills v. Green, 
159 U.S. 651
, 16 Sup. Ct. 132.
 Appeal dismissed.[ Board of Flour Inspectors for Port of New Orleans v. Glover

 (1895) ]
