 THE CHIEF JUSTICE.
 This is a writ of error, brought May 29, 1889, to an order overruling a motion to remand the case to the state court. Such an order is not a final judgment on the merits, and the writ of error must be dismissed. McLish v. Roff, 
141 U.S. 661
, 12 Sup. Ct. Rep. 118; Railway Co. v. Roberts, 
141 U.S. 690
, 12 Sup. Ct. Rep. 123; Joy v. Adelbert College, 
146 U.S. 355
, 13 Sup. Ct. Rep. 186.
