  Petition for writ of certiorari to the Court of Criminal Appeals of Texas.
  Denied.

 Justice BRENNAN and Justice MARSHALL dissenting:
  Adhering to our views that the death penalty is in all circumstances cruel and unusual punishment prohibited by the Eighth and Fourteenth Amendments, Gregg v. Georgia, 
428 
U.S. 153
, 227, 231, 2950, 2973 (1976), we would grant certiorari and vacate the death sentence in this case.
  Justice SCALIA took no part in the consideration or decision of this petition.[ McKay v. Texas
